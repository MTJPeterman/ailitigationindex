Methodology

The AI Litigation Index forecasts class action exposure by analyzing three layers:

  • Model Accuracy and Factuality - Data sourced from LMSYS Arena, HuggingFace, ARC Prize Challenge.
  • Sector Risk Adjustment - Sector multipliers based on harm probability (Healthcare 1.4x, Finance 1.3x, etc.).
  • Class Action Success Rates - Sector class action probabilities based on Stanford SCAC and Cornerstone Research data.

Final Risk Formula: Model Error × Sector Risk Multiplier × Class Action Success Rate

Column Definitions

  • Company: Public or major private company launching the AI product.
  • AI Product: Name of the AI system or service launched.
  • Launch Date: Public launch date or announcement date.
  • Model: AI model (e.g., GPT-4, Claude 3, Llama 3).
  • Wrong Answer Risk %: Estimated probability of hallucination or false output.
  • Real Harm Risk %: Probability that a wrong output causes material harm.
  • Class Action Success %: Historical probability of successful class actions in the sector.
  • Estimated Exposure ($): Predicted financial exposure based on potential litigation outcomes.
